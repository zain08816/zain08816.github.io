## Overview

**Fermi Paradox — illustrated walkthrough** is a **React + Vite + TypeScript** SPA organized into chapters. Each topic appears as a card; opening a card shows longer text, related links, and pointers into Wikipedia. Content follows Wikipedia’s structure with plain-language summaries and attribution in the app.

## Highlights

- Hash-based routing and `?segment=` links for sharing the current view on static hosts
- Reading progress for visited cards
